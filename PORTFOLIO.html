EARPHONE WITH AUTO ON AND OFF PROVISION THROUGH MONITORING BRAIN SIGNALS

An earphone device with auto on and off by monitoring brain signals comprises a headset, a pair of earphones with a UV lamp, a plurality of sensors to detect brain signals of a user, a location detection module , and a control unit comprises a microcontroller in communication with a mobile device in a Bluetooth network. The detected brain signals are transmitted to the mobile device to analyse the state of sleep of the brain and to switch on and off the device. UV lamps generate UV rays to kill bacteria in the ear.

DEVICE AND METHOD FOR DETECTING THE INFECTION STATE OF HAIR

The present disclosure relates to a device for detecting the infection state of the head part of the subject, the device includes a first set of sensors to capture images of the head part. The second set of sensors detects the accurate placement of the device in the head part and tests the quality of hair. A controller is configured to receive the captured images of the head part of the subject, receive the detected placement of the device and determine the quality of the hair of the subject, extract a set of attributes and compare the extracted set of attributes with reference set of attributes, where based on a combination of comparison, the controller is configured to determine a diagnosis for the head part of the subject.

WEARABLE DEVICE AND METHOD FOR GENERATING SOUND OF ANKLET BELL

A wearable device for generating a sound of anklet bells is disclosed. The wearable device provides a strap adapted to be worn around a leg of a user that comprises pressure sensors and motion sensors to detect an amount of pressure applied and a movement data of a foot of the user. The wearable device compiles the movement data and the amount of pressure using machine learning algorithms and creates a specific sound note. The wearable device further actuates a sound generating unit and a speaker to playback the sound of the anklet bells based on the created sound note. The wearable device is lightweight, portable, and audible to a mass audience.

COLOR BLIND AND MULTIUSABLE ELECTRIC TOOTHBRUSH

Based on the existing electric toothbrush design, we will replace the pressing button with a fingerprint sensor that vibrates when touched by the wrong owner and does not turn on and writes Welcome (name of owner) on the LED screen when touched by the right owner. This feature avoids misuse, especially for colourblind persons. A battery percentage feature will follow. Since electric toothbrushes don't have battery %, they display Dark Green for full battery, Yellow for half charge, and Red for no battery. Colourblind persons can't see colours, thus checking their toothbrush batteries is difficult. We replaced this with battery percentage, which users can observe and control toothbrush charging. An LED panel will display toothbrush information and remember last use on our electric toothbrush. GPS trackers help owners find misplaced items. Its processor regularly sends information to the user's smartphone, such as when they used it (to help parents watch their kids' brushing). Most people brush poorly, damaging their gums. The gyroscope helps users brush properly.
